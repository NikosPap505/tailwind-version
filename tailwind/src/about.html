About INVEST MCMM

Understanding the Micro-Credential Maturity Model and its impact on European Higher Education

Our Mission

INVEST MCMM aims to enhance the quality and recognition of micro-credentials in European higher education through a comprehensive maturity model that guides institutions toward excellence.

Our framework provides a structured approach for institutions to assess their current capabilities, identify areas for improvement, and implement best practices in micro-credential development and delivery.

The Maturity Model Framework

Instructional Design & Delivery

Focus on learning outcomes, assessment methods, and pedagogical approaches to ensure high-quality educational experiences.

Operational Infrastructure

Evaluate and improve the technical and administrative systems that support micro-credential programs.

Compliance & Governance

Ensure alignment with regulatory frameworks and maintain robust quality assurance processes.

Professional Development

Support educators and staff in developing the skills needed to deliver effective micro-credential programs.

Benefits of INVEST MCMM

For Institutions

  • Clear framework for program development
  • Structured assessment of current capabilities
  • Targeted recommendations for improvement
  • Alignment with European standards

For Learners

  • Higher quality educational experiences
  • Better recognition of credentials
  • Improved learning outcomes
  • Enhanced employability
